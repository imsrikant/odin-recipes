Frozen Peanut Butter Cheesecake

I'm not a cheesecake fan, unlike my husband, so this was my compromise. I actually really enjoy it!

Frozen Peanut Butter Cheesecake is a luscious dessert that combines the irresistible flavors of peanut butter and cream cheese.

This delectable treat starts with a buttery graham cracker crust as the foundation, providing a delightful contrast to the creamy filling.
