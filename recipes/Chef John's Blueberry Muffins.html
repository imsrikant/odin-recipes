Chef Jhon's Blueberry Muffins

I decided to push the envelope in this recipe for blueberry muffins and toss in 2 cups of blueberries!

Chef John's Blueberry Muffins are a mouthwatering treat that perfectly captures the essence of fresh, juicy blueberries. These muffins are incredibly moist and tender, with a delicate crumb that will leave you craving for more.

The recipe starts by combining flour, sugar, baking powder, and a pinch of salt in a mixing bowl. In a separate bowl, melted butter, milk, an egg, and a hint of vanilla extract are whisked together until well combined.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon grated lemon zest
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 2 cups fresh blueberries
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cups fresh blueberries
  • ¾ teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Step : 1

    2. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease 16 muffin cups, or line with paper muffin liners.

  2. Step : 2

    3. Beat butter and sugar in a large bowl until light and fluffy, about 3 minutes. Stir in eggs until well mixed. Add vegetable oil, stir to combine. Whisk sour cream, milk, and lemon zest into butter mixture until smooth.

  3. Step : 3

    4. Whisk flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a small bowl.

  4. Step : 4

    5. Stir half the flour mixture into the butter mixture until combined. Add remaining half of flour mixture and blueberries into the batter, folding together until just combined.

  5. Step : 5

    6. Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups.

  6. Step : 6

    7. Place muffin cups on center rack of the preheated oven and bake until tops are golden and a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean, about 30 minutes.
