Toasty Campfire Cookies

I created these cookies while camping, they are the tastiest campfire cookies you will ever eat! You can also make them at home over the stove.

Toasty Campfire Cookies are a delicious treat that can be made over a campfire or on the stove. The cookies are made with graham crackers, chocolate chips, and marshmallows. They are perfect for any occasion!

These cookies are so easy to make and they taste amazing! You can use any type of chocolate chip you want, but I prefer dark chocolate chips because they have a richer flavor than milk chocolate chips. If you don't have graham crackers on hand, you can use any type of cookie or cracker that you like.

Ingredients

  • 5 (1 ounce) squares chopped white chocolate
  • 20 oatmeal cookies
  • 1 (1.55 ounce) bar milk chocolate candy
  • 1 cup miniature marshmallows

Directions

  1. Step : 1

    2. Melt white chocolate in a double boiler, spread evenly over the bottoms of the cookies. Break up the milk chocolate bar into 10 pieces, and place 1 piece onto the white chocolate side of 10 cookies. Place the marshmallows on top of the milk chocolate. Top with the remaining cookies, creating a chocolate-marshmallow sandwich.

  2. Step : 2

    3. Toast cookies over a campfire, or stove using tongs until the chocolate and marshmallow has melted, about 3 minutes.
